Hokkien Mee Recipe

Savory Malaysian Noodles

Ingredients

  • 300g thick yellow noodles
  • 150g pork belly, sliced
  • 100g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100g cabbage, chopped
  • 100g bean sprouts
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 cup pork broth (or water)
  • Pork lard or cooking oil for frying
  • Optional: crispy pork lard bits for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat pork lard or oil in a wok. Saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced pork belly and stir-fry until lightly browned.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until just done. Set aside both.
  4. In the same wok, add noodles and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  5. Add cabbage and bean sprouts, then pour in broth.
  6. Return pork and shrimp to the wok. Add dark and light soy sauces.
  7. Stir everything well and cook until sauce thickens and noodles absorb flavor.
  8. Season with pepper. Garnish with crispy pork lard bits if desired.
  9. Serve hot with sambal and lime wedges.
