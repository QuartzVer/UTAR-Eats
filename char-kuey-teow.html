Char Kuey Teow Recipe

Malaysia's Iconic Wok-Fried Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g flat rice noodles (kuey teow)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 6‚Äì8 prawns (peeled and deveined)
  • Fish cake slices (optional)
  • 60g bean sprouts
  • Chives, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili paste (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Chinese sausage (lap cheong), sliced (optional)

Procedure

  1. Heat wok over high heat until smoking hot. Add oil and swirl to coat.
  2. Add garlic and stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add prawns and Chinese sausage. Stir-fry until prawns turn pink.
  4. Push to the side, crack in the eggs, and scramble briefly before mixing.
  5. Add noodles, light and dark soy sauces, and chili paste. Toss to coat evenly.
  6. Add bean sprouts and chives. Stir-fry for another 30‚Äì60 seconds, keeping everything moving.
  7. Serve hot immediately for the best wok hei (charred aroma) experience.
