Banana Leaf Rice Recipe

A South Indian Malaysian Feast

Ingredients

  • Steamed white rice
  • Banana leaves (washed and wiped)
  • Vegetable curry (e.g. dhal, sambar, or pumpkin curry)
  • Dry vegetable stir-fry (e.g. cabbage or long beans)
  • Pickles or acar (optional)
  • Papadums
  • Fried bitter gourd or brinjal (eggplant)
  • Coconut chutney or thuvaiyal
  • Spicy sambal
  • Chicken curry or mutton curry (optional for non-vegetarians)
  • Rasam (spiced soup)

Procedure

  1. Prepare banana leaves by cutting them into placemat-sized pieces. Lightly warm over a flame to make them flexible.
  2. Cook steamed white rice and keep it hot.
  3. Prepare accompanying vegetable curries and stir-fries in advance.
  4. Fry papadums and bitter gourd slices until crispy. Set aside.
  5. Lay out banana leaves on plates or table. Serve a heap of hot rice in the center.
  6. Pour a generous ladle of vegetable curry over the rice. Add vegetable sides around it.
  7. Add papadum, chutneys, and sambal as accompaniments. Serve rasam separately or pour over rice if desired.
  8. Optionally, include meat curry like chicken or mutton on the side.
  9. Eat with clean fingers and enjoy the authentic experience of Banana Leaf Rice!
