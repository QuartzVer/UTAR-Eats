Nasi Lemak Recipe

Malaysia's National Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 pandan leaf (tied into a knot)
  • Salt to taste
  • Boiled eggs
  • Fried anchovies (ikan bills)
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sambal (spicy chili paste)

Procedure

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. In a pot or rice cooker, add rice, coconut milk, pandan leaf, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Cook the rice as usual until soft and fluffy. Let it rest for a few minutes.
  4. While the rice is cooking, boil the eggs until fully cooked, then peel and slice them in half.
  5. Fry the anchovies until crispy and set aside.
  6. Roast the peanuts in a dry pan until golden and aromatic.
  7. Slice the cucumbers thinly.
  8. Prepare or heat the sambal chili paste (can be homemade or store-bought).
  9. Assemble the plate: scoop a portion of coconut rice in the center, and arrange egg, anchovies, peanuts, cucumber, and sambal around it.
  10. Serve warm and enjoy your Nasi Lemak!
