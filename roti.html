Roti Canai Recipe

Malaysia's Flaky Flatbread

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil or ghee (for frying and soaking)
  • Curry (for serving)

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar. Add egg, condensed milk, and enough water to form a soft dough.
  2. Knead the dough well until smooth and elastic. Divide into small balls and coat each with oil or ghee.
  3. Let the dough rest, covered, for at least 4 hours (overnight is better).
  4. Oil your hands and flatten each ball into a very thin sheet by stretching or rolling.
  5. Fold the edges inward to form a square or coil into a spiral and flatten again.
  6. Heat a skillet or griddle and cook the roti with ghee or oil until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  7. Serve hot with dhal curry, chicken curry, or sugar and condensed milk.
