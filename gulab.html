Gulab Jamun Recipe

Beloved Indian Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup milk (or as needed)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4-5 green cardamoms
  • Rose water or saffron strands (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee in a bowl.
  2. Add milk little by little and knead gently into a soft dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare sugar syrup: boil sugar, water, and cardamom until slightly sticky. Add rose water or saffron if using.
  4. Make small smooth balls from the dough without cracks.
  5. Heat oil or ghee on medium-low flame. Fry balls until golden brown evenly.
  6. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 30‚Äì45 minutes.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature, garnished with nuts if desired.
