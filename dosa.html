Masala Dosa Recipe

Classic Indian Delight

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for roasting
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • Chopped coriander leaves

Procedure

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6 hours. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  3. Add onions, ginger, turmeric, and saut√© until soft. Add mashed potatoes, salt, and coriander. Mix well to make the masala filling.
  4. Heat a flat pan or tawa. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
  5. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges and cook till crispy and golden.
  6. Place a portion of potato filling in the center and fold the dosa.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
