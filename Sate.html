Sate Recipe

Grilled Skewered Meat with Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken or beef, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bamboo skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes)
  • Peanut sauce for serving

Procedure

  1. Mix soy sauce, honey, turmeric powder, garlic, lemongrass, salt, and pepper in a bowl to make the marinade.
  2. Add the meat cubes to the marinade and coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the fridge.
  3. Thread the marinated meat onto the soaked bamboo skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium heat, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with peanut sauce and a side of cucumber and onions.
