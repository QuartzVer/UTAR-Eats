Pisang Goreng Recipe

Crispy Fried Bananas

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe bananas (pisang raja or plantains preferred)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust for batter consistency)
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedure

  1. Peel the bananas and slice them in half lengthwise.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, rice flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Gradually add water, mixing until you get a smooth, thick batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Dip each banana slice into the batter, ensuring it‚Äôs fully coated.
  6. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot as a delicious snack with tea or coffee.
