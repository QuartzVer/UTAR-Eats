Jianbing Recipe

Classic Chinese Street Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust for batter consistency)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 scallions, chopped
  • Cilantro (optional)
  • Crispy wonton sheets or fried crackers
  • Hoisin sauce or sweet bean sauce
  • Chili sauce (optional)
  • Sesame seeds

Procedure

  1. Mix flour and water to make a thin, smooth batter.
  2. Heat a flat pan or griddle, pour a thin layer of batter, spreading evenly.
  3. Crack an egg over the pancake and spread it evenly.
  4. Sprinkle chopped scallions, cilantro, and sesame seeds on top.
  5. Flip the pancake, add crispy wonton sheets, hoisin sauce, and chili sauce.
  6. Fold the pancake into halves or thirds and serve hot.
