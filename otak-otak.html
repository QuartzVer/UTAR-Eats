Otak-Otak Recipe

Grilled Fish Cake Wrapped in Banana Leaves

Ingredients

  • 500g mackerel fish fillet (boneless)
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp chili paste
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 5 shallots (minced)
  • 1 stalk lemongrass (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • Banana leaves (softened over heat)

Procedure

  1. Blend fish fillet into a smooth paste.
  2. Mix fish paste with coconut milk, chili paste, garlic, shallots, lemongrass, sugar, salt, and egg until well combined.
  3. Cut banana leaves into rectangles and soften them by briefly passing over an open flame.
  4. Place a few tablespoons of mixture in the center of each banana leaf piece.
  5. Fold and secure the banana leaves with toothpicks.
  6. Grill over medium heat for about 5‚Äì7 minutes per side until fragrant and cooked through.
  7. Serve hot as a snack or side dish.
