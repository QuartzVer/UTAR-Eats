Tanghulu Recipe

Chinese Candied Fruit on a Stick

Ingredients

  • Fresh strawberries, grapes, or hawthorn berries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • Skewers or bamboo sticks
  • Parchment paper or silicone mat

Procedure

  1. Wash and dry the fruit thoroughly. Any moisture will prevent the sugar from sticking.
  2. Skewer 2‚Äì4 fruits onto each stick and set aside on a clean tray.
  3. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat over medium heat without stirring.
  4. Let it boil until the syrup reaches a light golden color (approx. 300¬∞F or hard crack stage).
  5. Carefully dip each fruit skewer into the syrup and rotate to coat evenly.
  6. Place coated skewers on parchment paper or a silicone mat to cool and harden.
  7. Once the sugar coating is crisp and clear, serve immediately and enjoy!
