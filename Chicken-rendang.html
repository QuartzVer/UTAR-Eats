Rendang Chicken Recipe

Rich and Fragrant Malaysian Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 stalks lemongrass (bruised)
  • 4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 turmeric leaves (optional, sliced thinly)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Spice paste (blend together):
  • 6 shallots
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 5 cm ginger
  • 5 cm galangal
  • 5 cm fresh turmeric
  • 8‚Äì10 dried chilies (soaked and deseeded)

Procedure

  1. Blend the spice paste ingredients until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and fry the spice paste until aromatic.
  3. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and turmeric leaves (if using).
  4. Stir in the chicken pieces and cook until they are coated with the paste.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and tamarind paste. Stir well.
  6. Simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. Cook until the chicken is tender and the sauce thickens to a dry, rich consistency.
  8. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice or lemang.
